Work history

  • I am currently Product Lead at Probable Futures. We create digital platforms that offer interactive maps, science, historical context, and stories to help us all envision a range of climate futures. I work across organizations with a design team collaboration between Postlight and Moth Design, an engineering team at Postlight, and the Risk team at Woodwell Climate Research Center.
  • Probable Futures maps are part of ProbableFutures.org and also are part of additional applications that are in R&D. The global maps show past, present, future, and potential climate conditions paired with stories to contextualize what the changes mean.
  • An anonymous climate change Q&A forum for Lowercarbon Capital.
  • Agent Portal, an insurance quoting engine and policy management system for insurance agents who sell SageSure homeowners insurance for Insight Catastrophe Group.
  • MySageSure, a policyholder account management web application for Insight Catastrophe Group.
  • Find an Agent, a directory using the Google Maps API to help homeowners find SageSure insurance agents nearby.
  • Original Content, a CMS and company blog for Dollar Shave Club.
  • MEL Magazine, a CMS and online magazine for Dollar Shave Club.
  • The Great American Read website, an online index of books and a voting application for the PBS show The Great American Read.

Formal bio

Peter leads digital strategy and software product teams. He focuses on climate change and works as an independent consultant or contractor. He currently serves as Product Lead at Probable Futures where he works with scientists, designers, and software engineers to publish interactive maps, science, historical context, and stories to help us all envision a range of climate futures.
