Work history

  • Probable Futures is an editorial starting place for learning about our changing world. It includes four volumes covering the topics we will all need to understand to live well with climate instability. Designed in collaboration with Moth Design.
  • Probable Futures public maps is a collection of global maps showing past, present, future, and potential climate conditions paired with stories to contextualize what the changes mean. Built as an accessible public utility with data and advisory from Woodwell Climate Research Center.
  • An anonymous climate change Q&A forum for Lowercarbon Capital.
  • Agent Portal, an insurance quoting engine and policy management system for insurance agents who sell SageSure homeowners insurance for Insight Catastrophe Group.
  • MySageSure, a policyholder account management web application for Insight Catastrophe Group.
  • Find an Agent, a directory using the Google Maps API to help homeowners find SageSure insurance agents nearby.
  • Original Content, a CMS and company blog for Dollar Shave Club.
  • MEL Magazine, a CMS and online magazine for Dollar Shave Club.
  • The Great American Read website, an online index of books and a voting application for the PBS show The Great American Read.
  • Audubon Bird Guide, an iOS and Android birdwatching app for The Audubon Society.

Formal bio

Peter leads digital strategy and software product teams. He focuses on climate change and works as an independent consultant or contractor. Peter is the product lead for Probable Futures where he works with scientists, designers, and software engineers to make tools that visualize climate change along with stories and insights we will all need to understand to live well with climate instability.
